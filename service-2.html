What is OEM Advertising?

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) advertising is a powerful way to promote your APK apps through on-device advertising.

Instead of relying solely on traditional app stores, OEM advertising places your app directly in front of users through:

  • Pre-installed apps
  • In-device app recommendations
  • Alternative app stores run by device manufacturers

This approach ensures your app gets seen by the right users at the right time, driving installs and engagement without the delays of traditional platforms.

Who is This Service For?

OEM advertising is perfect for developers and marketers looking to promote:

  • Financial Apps
  • Gaming and Entertainment Apps
  • Betting and Gambling Apps
  • Niche or regional Apps

If your app struggles with strict moderation or approval delays on platforms like Google Play or the App Store, OEM advertising offers a seamless and effective alternative.
